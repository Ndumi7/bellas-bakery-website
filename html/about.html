Our History

Bella’s Artisan Bakery was founded in 2018 in Cape Town by Bella, a passionate baker who grew up learning the art of breadmaking and pastry from her grandmother. What started as a small kitchen project quickly grew into a beloved community bakery, serving families, tourists, and local cafés.

Since then, Bella’s Bakery has become a trusted name for fresh, handmade breads, cakes, and pastries crafted with ingredients sourced from local farmers.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To make tasty food with love, using fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Vision: To be the top bakery in Cape Town, known for quality, creativity, and community spirit.
