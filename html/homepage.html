Welcome to Bella’s Artisan Bakery

At Bella’s, we believe in the magic of fresh, handmade baking. From our crusty sourdough breads to indulgent cakes and buttery pastries, every bite is made with love and care.

Founded in 2018, our bakery has grown to become a favorite in Cape Town for families, tourists, and event planners looking for something special.

What We Offer

  • Freshly baked artisan breads, daily.
  • Custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and celebrations.
  • Delicious pastries, tarts, and croissants.
  • Friendly service with a warm community feel.
